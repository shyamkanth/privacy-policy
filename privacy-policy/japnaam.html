Japnaam - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 9, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Japnaam. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app manages your data, including what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

2. Information We Collect

Japnaam is designed to help you track your jaap (chant counts), access a wide list of aartis, and manage your spiritual practices. We do not collect or store any personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

All your jaap counts, preferences, and notes are stored locally on your device. This information is never uploaded to our servers.

3. Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage information such as crash reports and app performance data. This is done only to improve the stability and experience of the app. No personal or sensitive information is collected in this process.

4. How We Use Information

The information stored on your device is used to:

- Track and display your jaap counts.
- Provide you access to aartis and related spiritual content.
- Enhance your app experience with personalized features.
- Fix bugs and improve app performance through crash reports.

5. Data Security

We prioritize your privacy and data security. All jaap counts and related data are stored locally, ensuring that you remain in full control of your information. Since the app does not require internet to function, your data stays private.

6. Data Sharing

Japnaam does not share, sell, or transmit your personal information or jaap counts to any third parties.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party libraries, such as Firebase, for crash reporting. These services collect only non-identifiable information necessary for improving the app's stability.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the app and on this page with a revised effective date.
